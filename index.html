When Axie Infinity exploded in the Philippines, I didn't just play — I built a guild. RBR gave scholarships to people who couldn't afford to start. I managed scholars, split earnings fairly, and helped families earn real income during the pandemic. It wasn't about getting rich — it was about helping people who needed it.

2021-2025 — The World
Seeing the World

Traveled to many countries across Asia, South America, and beyond. Each place changed how I see the world — how communities work, how trust is built, and how technology connects people everywhere.

✈️ World Traveler
February 2026
CebuLandMarket — Making Property Fair

Saw how sketchy buying land in Cebu can be — fake titles, shady brokers, zero transparency. So I built CebuLandMarket: verified listings, document checks, real due diligence. DTI-registered. Because Filipinos deserve honest property deals.

🏡 DTI Registered
March 2026
Townik — Your Town's Network

Built Townik because finding trusted help in Cebu was broken. People posting on Facebook groups, praying someone legit responds. Townik has 36+ service categories, ID-verified workers, instant booking. One app to find help you can actually trust.

🌐 36+ Services ✅ ID Verified
2026

Launched a Cebuano-themed lifestyle brand. Caps, tees, and accessories that celebrate what it means to be Bisaya. Because our culture deserves to be worn with pride — not just spoken.
