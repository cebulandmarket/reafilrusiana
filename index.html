Who I Am

About Me

I'm Reafil B. Rusiana — known to friends as Brodit Reafil. I'm a Filipino entrepreneur, traveler, and self-taught tech builder based in Cebu, Philippines.

I grew up in Cebu with a curiosity for how things work and a drive to create something meaningful. Instead of waiting for opportunities, I built them — launching three businesses in 2026 alone, all from scratch, all self-taught.

But I'm not just about work. I'm someone who believes life is meant to be experienced. I've traveled to Japan, Hong Kong, Colombia, and other countries — exploring cultures, meeting people, and bringing those perspectives back into everything I build.

I'm a Bisaya at heart. Proud of where I come from, and driven to put Cebu on the map — not just as a tourist destination, but as a place where real innovation happens.

Whether it's coding a platform at 2AM, exploring the streets of Tokyo, or helping a seller list their land — I show up fully.
